Let's Work Together

Speedball Health Club is a family run Bradford business with you in mind. Open until 12am, 7 days a week, we make sure our times suit you and your needs. We will work with you to help you achieve your fitness goals through every stretch, pull, lift, jog and step of the way. We pledge to share our fitness expertise and dedicate our efforts to help you succeed.

Take Control of Your Fitness

Here at Speedball you never have to get stuck in an old routine. We offer a variety of classes and clubs for you to get involved with. We encourage all our members to take part in as many fitness classes as possible. There is something here for everyone, any age, any level of fitness.

Inspiring You to Succeed

We are here to help you tone, strengthen, burn and energise your soul until you are happy with your body and have reached your fitness goals. Then, if you like, we can set you some more! Our aim is to inspire and motivate you with our fitness knowledge and exercise expertise by not only to helping you workout, but by also advising you on your diet and nutrition as well.
